Chiayi

A nature-focused region home to forests, tea plantations, and highland railways.

Chiayi City (嘉義, Jiāyì) is a small city in southern Taiwan, serving as a gateway to Alishan. The city combines historical architecture, lively markets, and cultural attractions with a relaxed urban pace. Known for its railway culture and local delicacies, Chiayi City offers visitors access to nearby natural wonders and an authentic experience of southern Taiwanese life.
