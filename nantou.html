Nantou

A mountainous heartland featuring scenic lakes and indigenous culture.

Nantou (南投, Nántóu) is the only landlocked county in Taiwan, located in the central mountains. Known for its scenic landscapes, including Sun Moon Lake and Hehuanshan, Nantou is a hub for outdoor recreation, tea plantations, and cultural experiences. The county combines natural beauty with indigenous and traditional Taiwanese culture, making it a popular destination for relaxation, sightseeing, and nature exploration.
