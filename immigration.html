Taiwan Tourist Visa Requirements 2025

Complete guide to tourist visa requirements for Taiwan. Learn about visa applications, required documents, processing times, and fees.

Visa Requirements

To apply for a tourist visa to Taiwan, you will need the following documents:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings or invitation letter)
  • Return flight tickets
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Travel insurance (may be required)
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate (if arriving from endemic areas)

Visa Duration

Tourist visas typically allow stays of 30-90 days, depending on nationality and visa type.

Application Tips

Follow these important guidelines to ensure a smooth visa application process:

  • Apply at least 2-4 weeks before your planned travel date
  • Ensure all documents are translated to English or the local language
  • Some countries offer visa on arrival or e-visa options for tourists
  • Check if your nationality is eligible for visa-free entry
  • Keep copies of all submitted documents

Residence - Long-Term Stays

For long-term stays in Taiwan, the duration of residency varies based on the purpose of the application, typically ranging from 6 months to 3 years. Extension of residency is permitted if foreigners meet the relevant requirements.

How to Apply for Residence

  1. Foreigners may apply for a "visa" through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs or R.O.C. Embassies and Missions Abroad if they meet the relevant requirements. After entering Taiwan with the visa for specific reasons, they can apply for an "Alien Resident Certificate (ARC)" with the National Immigration Agency (NIA).
  2. The validity of the ARC varies depending on the reason for the application and generally ranges from 6 months to 3 years.
  3. Extension or change of residence purpose is applicable if foreigners comply with the relevant requirements.
