Hsinchu

A technology-driven city balanced by historic temples and coastal scenery.

Hsinchu (新竹, Xīnzhú) is a high-tech city in northern Taiwan, famous for its Hsinchu Science and Industrial Park. Despite its modern technology focus, the city retains traditional charm through historic temples, old streets, and local cuisine, such as rice noodles and pork balls. Known as “The Windy City” for its frequent breezes, Hsinchu combines innovation, culture, and culinary delights, offering visitors a unique blend of heritage and modernity.
