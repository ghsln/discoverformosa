Changhua

A historic gateway city leading to Taiwan’s famous mountain landscapes.

Changhua (彰化, Zhānghuà) is a historic city in central Taiwan, known for its rich cultural heritage, temples, and traditional crafts. The city blends urban life with agricultural landscapes, offering visitors scenic countryside views alongside lively markets and festivals. Famous for attractions like Baguashan Buddha and traditional Taiwanese snacks, Changhua presents a unique combination of history, culture, and local charm in the heart of Taiwan.
