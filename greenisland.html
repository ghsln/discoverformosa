Green Island

A volcanic island known for coral reefs and natural hot springs.

Green Island (綠島, Lüdao) is a volcanic island off the eastern coast of Taiwan, renowned for its turquoise waters, coral reefs, and hot springs. The island offers excellent diving, snorkeling, and outdoor recreation, combining natural beauty with a laid-back island atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy scenic landscapes, local culture, and marine life, making Green Island a popular getaway.
