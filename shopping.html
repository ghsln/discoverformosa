Shopping in Taiwan

From high-end malls to bustling markets, Taiwan offers a diverse shopping experience. Whether you're looking for luxury brands, trendy fashion, local souvenirs, or delicious street food, you'll find it in Taiwan's vibrant shopping destinations.

Night Markets

The heart of Taiwanese shopping and street food.

Shilin Night Market
(士林夜市)

View on Maps

The largest and most famous night market in Taipei, selling everything from clothes and gadgets to iconic Taiwanese snacks. A must-visit for any shopping and food enthusiast.

Raohe Street Night Market
(饒河夜市)

View on Maps

A more traditional night market with a fantastic selection of food and a famous pepper bun stall at the entrance. Located along Raohe Street in Taipei's Songshan District.

Fengjia Night Market
(逢甲夜市)

View on Maps

One of the largest and most innovative night markets in Taiwan, located in Taichung. Known for trendy fashion, creative street food, and a youthful atmosphere.

Malls and Outlets

For those looking for a more upscale shopping experience.

Xinyi District (信義區), Taipei

Taipei's upscale shopping hub, home to malls like Taipei 101 Mall, Breeze Xinyi, and Shin Kong Mitsukoshi. This area features luxury brands, department stores, cinemas, and fine dining restaurants all within walking distance.

GLORIA OUTLETS (華泰名品城), Taoyuan

Taiwan's first open-air, luxury-brand outlet mall, located near Taoyuan HSR station. Features international designer brands at discounted prices with a pleasant outdoor shopping environment.

Mitsui Outlet Park (三井 Outlet)

Large Japanese-style outlet malls with locations in both Taichung and Taipei (Linkou). Offers a great mix of international brands, restaurants, entertainment, and family-friendly facilities.

Department Stores & Boutiques

Beyond malls and outlets, Taiwan has numerous department stores like Sogo, Shin Kong Mitsukoshi, and Far Eastern Department Stores scattered throughout major cities, offering everything from luxury goods to daily necessities.
