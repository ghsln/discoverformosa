Unique Stays in Taiwan

For a truly memorable experience beyond standard hotels, consider a stay at a leisure farm or themed resort. These are particularly popular for families or those seeking a deeper connection with Taiwan's nature and culture.

Note: Each unique stay offers distinctive amenities, locations, and experiences. Whether you're seeking a floral paradise, ecological immersion, culinary creativity, or historic European charm, these stays deliver unforgettable memories and deeper connections with Taiwan.

Miaoli

Miaoli Flower Home Leisure Farm

A charming flower farm stay with only 18 exclusive rooms. It offers a resort-like feel with amenities like in-room bath bombs, aromatherapy foot soaks, and DIY activities such as making tea tree essential oil moisturizer.

The intimate setting provides a peaceful retreat surrounded by beautiful floral landscapes. Guests can enjoy personalized attention and participate in hands-on activities that connect them with local botanical products.

Taiyi Ecological Leisure Farm

Offers a unique choice between a modern resort and vintage single floating cabins over a pond. The farm also features environmentally friendly systems and beautiful gardens to explore.

This ecological haven allows guests to choose their preferred style of accommodation while being surrounded by sustainable practices and natural beauty. The floating cabins provide a particularly unique experience connected directly to the water environment.

Near Kaohsiung

Fuwan Villa

A modern paradise known for its award-winning chocolate and seafood. You can learn about chocolate making and even create your own sakura prawn chocolates in their workshop.

Combining culinary excellence with comfortable accommodations, Fuwan Villa offers a unique experience where guests can indulge in gourmet creations while learning the art of chocolate making. The seafood offerings provide a delicious taste of local flavors.

Old England Manor

This manor is designed to resemble a building from 18th or 19th century England, complete with antique decorations flown in from England. It offers a high level of privacy and unique facilities like the Shakespeare Hall, which transforms into an English bar at night.

Transport yourself to historical England without leaving Taiwan. The authentic decorations and architectural details create an immersive experience, while the Shakespeare Hall adds a unique social element that changes from day to evening.
