DAY-BY-DAY PLAN

Day 1: Arrival in Taipei

▼

Morning

  • Arrive at Taoyuan Airport
  • Take Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (35 mins, NT$160)
  • Check into hotel in Ximending area

Afternoon

  • Visit Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall
  • Explore Ximending shopping district
  • Try beef noodles at Yong Kang Beef Noodles

Evening

  • Shilin Night Market for dinner and snacks
  • Must-try: oyster omelet, stinky tofu, bubble tea

Day 2: Taipei City Exploration

▼

Morning

  • Visit Taipei 101 Observatory (opens 9 AM)
  • Elephant Mountain hike for city views (1 hour)

Afternoon

  • National Palace Museum (2-3 hours)
  • Lunch at Din Tai Fung (xiaolongbao)
  • Walk around Yongkang Street area

Evening

  • Raohe Night Market
  • Try pepper buns and medicinal pork ribs soup

Day 3: Jiufen & Northeast Coast

▼

Morning

  • Take bus 1062 from Taipei Main Station to Jiufen (90 mins)
  • Explore Jiufen Old Street
  • Visit A Mei Tea House

Afternoon

  • Lunch: taro balls with sweet soup
  • Walk to Jiufen Viewpoint
  • Optional: Visit nearby Shifen for sky lanterns

Evening

  • Return to Taipei
  • Ningxia Night Market for lighter evening snacks

Day 4: Taichung Day Trip

▼

Morning

  • Take HSR to Taichung (1 hour, NT$700)
  • Visit Rainbow Village (colorful street art)

Afternoon

  • Miyahara Ice Cream (historic building)
  • Explore Fengjia Night Market (largest in Taiwan)
  • Try: grilled squid, fried king mushroom

Evening

  • Return to Taipei on HSR
  • Rest at hotel

Day 5: Departure Day

▼

Morning

  • Last-minute shopping at Taipei Station Underground Mall
  • Pick up pineapple cakes as souvenirs

Afternoon

  • Check out from hotel
  • Take Airport MRT to Taoyuan Airport
  • Arrive 3 hours before international flight
