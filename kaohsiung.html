Kaohsiung

A modern harbor city blending art, industry, and coastal charm.

Kaohsiung (高雄, Gāoxióng) is a major port city in southern Taiwan, combining industrial development with coastal scenery and modern urban life. Known for its harbor, vibrant night markets, and cultural attractions, Kaohsiung offers waterfront parks, historic sites, and artistic neighborhoods. With a warm climate, lively streets, and a strong maritime identity, the city is a key center for commerce, culture, and tourism in southern Taiwan.
