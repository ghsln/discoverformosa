Essential Apps for Your Taiwan Adventure

Navigating Taiwan is incredibly easy with a smartphone. Having the right apps will transform your travel experience, helping you with everything from finding the next high-speed train to translating a menu. Here are the must-have applications for any visitor.

Tip: Download these apps before your trip and set up any necessary accounts. For transportation apps like T-Express, you'll need to register with a credit card to purchase tickets.

Navigation & Translation

These apps will help you find your way around Taiwan and overcome language barriers, making your travels smoother and more enjoyable.

Google Maps
Navigation

This is your absolute essential. It provides accurate GPS navigation, detailed public transportation routes and schedules (including bus arrival times), and comprehensive business listings. You can even download offline maps for areas with spotty service.

Transportation

Taiwan has an excellent transportation network. These apps will help you navigate trains, buses, and ride-sharing services with ease.

Taiwan High Speed Rail (THSR) App (T-Express)
Trains

The official app for booking tickets on Taiwan's bullet train. You can purchase, change, and refund tickets, and most importantly, use a QR code on your phone as your ticket, saving you time at the station.

Taiwan Railway App
Trains

For journeys along the scenic east coast or to smaller cities not served by the High-Speed Rail, this app is perfect. It provides schedules, fares, and booking for the comprehensive Taiwan Railway Administration (TRA) network.
