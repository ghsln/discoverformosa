Yunlin

A rural county known for agriculture, traditional crafts, and local faith culture.

Yunlin (雲林, Yúnlín) is a county in western Taiwan known for its fertile agricultural lands, traditional villages, and vibrant local culture. Famous for farming, night markets, and temples, Yunlin offers visitors a glimpse into rural Taiwanese life. Cultural festivals, local delicacies, and scenic landscapes highlight the county’s unique identity as a center for agriculture and heritage in western Taiwan.
