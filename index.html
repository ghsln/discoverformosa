Let's Explore
Taiwan!

Cities and Counties

  • City

    Taipei

    Taipei is Taiwan's capital and the center of culture, politics, and business. It's known for landmarks like Taipei 101 and its vibrant night markets.

  • City

    New Taipei

    New Taipei surrounds Taipei and offers a mix of urban life and nature. It includes famous spots like Jiufen and Yehliu Geopark.

  • City

    Taoyuan

    Taoyuan City is a major transport and industrial hub that hosts Taiwan’s largest international airport.

  • City/County

    Hsinchu

    Hsinchu is known as Taiwan's tech hub due to the Science Park. Windy weather and rice noodles are two of its trademarks.

  • City

    Keelung

    Keelung is a rainy port city in northern Taiwan. Its Miaokou Night Market is popular with visitors.

  • City

    Yilan

    Yilan offers lush mountains, hot springs, and a slower lifestyle. It's popular for its festivals and scenic coastline.

  • County

    Miaoli

    Miaoli is another strong Hakka region with peaceful rural scenery. Visitors enjoy activities like strawberry picking in winter.

  • City/County

    Taichung

    Taichung is known for its pleasant climate and creative atmosphere. It features attractions like the National Taichung Theater and Miyahara.

  • County

    Changhua

    Changhua County is a west-central Taiwan county known for its fertile agricultural plains, rich cultural heritage, and historic towns like Lukang.

  • County

    Yunlin

    Yunlin County is an agricultural county in western central Taiwan on the Chianan Plain, known for its fertile farmland and rural culture.

  • City/County

    Chiayi

    Chiayi is a city in southwestern Taiwan known for its historic charm, cultural sites, and as a gateway to the scenic Alishan mountains.

  • City

    Tainan

    Tainan is a historic city in southwestern Taiwan, known as the island’s oldest city with rich cultural heritage, temples, and famous local food.

  • County

    Nantou

    Nantou County is Taiwan’s only landlocked county, known for Sun Moon Lake, mountains, and lush natural scenery.

  • City

    Kaohsiung

    Kaohsiung is a major port city and special municipality in southern Taiwan, known for its busy harbor, industry, and cultural attractions.

  • County

    Pingtung

    Pingtung County is a southern Taiwan county known for its tropical climate, agriculture, and Kenting National Park.

  • County

    Hualien

    Hualien is an eastern Taiwan county on the Pacific coast, famous for natural scenery like Taroko Gorge and the East Rift Valley.

  • County

    Taitung

    Taitung County is a sparsely populated southeastern Taiwan county known for its natural scenery, Pacific coast, and rich indigenous culture.

  • Island

    Green Island

    Green Island is a volcanic island off the eastern coast of Taiwan, known for its diving spots and beautiful coral reefs.

  • Island

    Liuqiu Island

    Liuqiu Island, also called Xiaoliuqiu, is a small coral island in southwestern Taiwan, popular for snorkeling and sea turtle sightings.

  • Island

    Orchid Island

    Orchid Island is a remote island off Taiwan’s southeast coast, home to the indigenous Tao people and renowned for its lush scenery and diving spots.

  • County

    Kinmen

    Kinmen County is a small Taiwanese county made up of islands near mainland China, known for its rich history, military heritage, and unique local culture and scenery.

  • County

    Penghu

    Penghu County is a group of about 90 islands in the Taiwan Strait, known for its beaches, basalt landscapes, and maritime culture.
