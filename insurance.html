Travel Insurance in Taiwan

Travel insurance in Taiwan refers to a voluntary insurance policy you can purchase to protect yourself against unexpected events during travel, such as emergency medical treatment, accidents, trip cancellations, lost baggage, or delays. Many Taiwanese insurance companies offer 旅遊平安險 (travel safety insurance) that provides coverage for accidental injury, medical expenses, and other travel-related risks, and plans can often be bought online, through travel agencies, or sometimes at airport insurance counters before departure.

Common Insurance

旅遊平安險
(Travel Safety/Accident Insurance)
Covers accidental injury
Covers accidental death or disability
Emergency medical treatment
旅遊不便險
(Travel Inconvenience/Trip Protection Insurance)
Compensation for flight delays or cancellations
Compensation for baggage delay or loss
For trip interruption or change and travel document loss

Additional Coverage

Overseas emergency medical/dental care (especially important for travel abroad)

Emergency rescue services (medical evacuation, repatriation, etc.)

Third-party liability (if you accidentally injure someone or damage property)

Optional add-ons or riders, like coverage for legal assistance or special risks (varies by policy)

How to Buy

Directly from Taiwanese insurance companies (e.g., Fubon, Cathay, Taiwan Property & Casualty)

Online through comparison or insurer websites

Sometimes through credit cards or travel agencies (though credit card coverage may be limited)

Airport insurance counters before departure
