Budget Accommodations in Taiwan

Taiwan offers excellent budget accommodation options that provide comfort, convenience, and unique experiences without breaking the bank. From world-class hostels to stylish budget hotels, these properties prove that affordability doesn't mean sacrificing quality or memorable stays.

Note: Each accommodation featured below offers excellent value, combining affordability with unique features, prime locations, and quality amenities. Whether you're a solo traveler, backpacker, or budget-conscious tourist, Taiwan's budget accommodations deliver comfortable and memorable stays.

Taipei

Star Hostel Taipei Main Station

Often cited as one of the best hostels in the world, Star Hostel offers far more than a typical budget stay. Its prime location just a short walk from Taipei Main Station provides unparalleled access to trains, the MRT, and buses to every corner of the island.

The hostel is renowned for its spacious, light-filled common area with large wooden tables, a shared kitchen, and a self-service laundry facility. With a free simple breakfast, friendly and helpful staff, and both dormitory and private room options, it provides a social, clean, and incredibly convenient base for exploring Taipei.

Taichung

Hotel Euphemia

Perfect for foodies, Hotel Euphemia is strategically located less than a kilometer from the famous Fengjia Night Market, one of Taiwan's largest and most vibrant.

This modern hotel strikes a great balance between affordability and style, featuring an on-site bar and a fitness center. The rooms are clean, comfortable, and equipped with air conditioning and free WiFi.

Kaohsiung

Fullon Poshtel - Kaohsiung

Breaking the mold of a standard hostel, Fullon Poshtel in Kaohsiung's Qianjin District offers a "poshtel" experience. It blends the social and affordable aspects of a hostel with the amenities of a higher-end hotel.

Guests can enjoy a fitness centre, a restaurant, and a shared lounge, which are rare finds in budget accommodations. With its modern design and convenient facilities like a concierge and tour desk, it serves as a perfect, stylish hub for exploring Kaohsiung's art centers, Pier-2 Art District, and Ruifeng Night Market.

Taipei

Meander Taipei Hostel

Situated in the heart of the bustling Ximending pedestrian shopping district, Meander Hostel is the go-to choice for travelers who want to be in the middle of the action. Ximending is packed with shops, street food stalls, theaters, and cafes.

The hostel itself is modern, secure, and features a vibrant common area that makes it easy to meet other travelers. They offer both dorms and private rooms, along with helpful services like luggage storage and a shared kitchen. It's the perfect spot for young travelers looking for an energetic and central Taipei experience.

Hualien

Journey Hostel Hualien

For adventurers using Hualien as a gateway to the breathtaking Taroko Gorge, Journey Hostel is a top-tier choice. More like a boutique guesthouse than a simple hostel, it boasts a clean, minimalist design and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

The staff are exceptionally knowledgeable and can assist with arranging scooter rentals and permits for visiting Taroko National Park. With comfortable common areas perfect for relaxing after a day of hiking and both dorm and private rooms available, it provides a peaceful and social home base for exploring Taiwan's stunning east coast.
