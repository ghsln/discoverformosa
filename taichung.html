Taichung

A cultural and creative hub blending art, food, and modern city life

Taichung (台中, Táizhōng) is a vibrant city in central Taiwan, combining modern urban life with cultural and artistic attractions. Known for its lively night markets, parks, museums, and street art, Taichung attracts both locals and tourists. The city offers diverse culinary experiences, historic temples, and scenic spots such as Gaomei Wetlands, making it a center for culture, entertainment, and natural beauty in central Taiwan.
