Welcome to Taiwan's Special Travel Guides

Taiwan is an incredibly welcoming destination for all types of travelers. Whether you're traveling with family, exploring solo, following dietary restrictions, or have accessibility needs, Taiwan offers exceptional experiences tailored to your journey. This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the island with confidence and ease.

Family Travel in Taiwan

Taiwan is extremely family-friendly, offering discounted fares for children, kid-friendly facilities, and numerous attractions perfect for families.

Child Fares

Taiwan High-Speed Rail (THSR)

  • Children (6-11 years): 50% off

Accessibility Guide

Taiwan has made significant strides in accessibility, especially in major cities.

Transportation Accessibility

MRT (Metro)

  • All stations have elevators
  • Tactile guiding paths throughout
  • Priority seating available
