Orchid Island

A remote island preserving indigenous culture and dramatic seascapes.

Orchid Island (蘭嶼, Lányǔ) is a remote island southeast of Taiwan, home to the indigenous Tao people. Known for its rugged cliffs, pristine beaches, and unique cultural heritage, Orchid Island offers a serene and immersive experience. Visitors can explore traditional villages, natural landscapes, and marine life, gaining insight into the island’s rich indigenous culture.
